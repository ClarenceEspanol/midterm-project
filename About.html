VISION STATEMENT

Our Lady of Lourdes College Foundation is a learning institution, holistically responsive and globally competitive, producing graduates who are competent, morally upright, and productive citizens.

MISSION STATEMENT

Our Lady of Lourdes College Foundation, as an institution of higher learning is dedicated to the pursuit of Knowledge, Truth and Wisdom through the Guidance of the Divine Providence as it seeks the total development and formation of the Filipino Youth.

PHILOSOPHY

The academic programs of Our Lady of Lourdes College Foundation are geared towards empowering its students with human, conceptual and technical skills so that they can best develop themselves and be instruments in bringing about a good and fruitful life for all.

OBJECTIVES

Our Lady of Lourdes College Foundation commits itself to make every Lourdenian graduate:

  1. A productive Filipino Citizen

  2. A citizen who is committed to uplift the quality of life of other people, and,

  3. A fully developed and globally competitive professional who respects the personal worth and dignity of people and is committed to the development of the community and country.
